## ARRIVAL WEEK — ACTION LIST (Priority Order)

### Day 1–3
- [ ] Check into accommodation
- [ ] Buy local SIM card (Telekom or Vodafone — best coverage)
  - Bring Indian passport + address proof
- [ ] Withdraw cash from ATM (Sparkasse ATMs free for most cards)
- [ ] Buy 1-week transit pass for the city

### Day 3–7: Legal Requirements
- [ ] **Anmeldung (Address Registration)** — MANDATORY within 2 weeks
  - Go to Einwohnermeldeamt or Bürgerbüro
  - Bring: Passport + Opportunity Card + accommodation confirmation letter from landlord (Wohnungsgeberbestätigung)
  - Free to register
  - You receive a Meldebescheinigung (registration certificate) — keep this, you need it for bank account, insurance, etc.

- [ ] **Open German Bank Account**
  - N26 (online, easy for internationals, English app)
  - Deutsche Bank (traditional, more branches)
  - Need: Passport + Anmeldung certificate

- [ ] **Health Insurance**
  - While job searching (not employed): Private health insurance (Expat / international policy)
  - Budget: €150–250/month (get pre-existing condition cover confirmed in writing)
  - Recommended: Feather Insurance, Ottonova, DR-WALTER (English-friendly)
  - Once employed: Switch to statutory GKV — employer covers 50%

### Week 2

- [ ] Get German prescription for diabetes medication
- [ ] Update LinkedIn location to your German city

## OPPORTUNITY CARD → BLUE CARD CONVERSION (After Job Offer)

Once you receive a job offer in Germany:

1. Job offer letter with salary ≥ €45,300/year (IT shortage occupation) → Blue Card eligible
2. Employer applies for your work permit / Blue Card at Ausländerbehörde (immigration office)
3. You get a temporary Fiktionsbescheinigung (fictional certificate) to continue working legally
4. Blue Card issued — usually within 4–8 weeks
5. Blue Card → Permanent Residency path:
   - Standard: 33 months with Blue Card
   - With B1 German language: 21 months
   - Citizenship possible after 5–8 years

**Your salary target €90,000–100,000 is well above Blue Card threshold — you qualify easily.**
